Flu reporter

Past few years especially in flu seasons, I used to use Google FLu Trend to check the flu trend in Sydney area. After Google stopped this service on September 2015, I realized the similar data still could be found on Google Trend. However, on Google trend page you have to set category, country, region, time … to get result. That’s a bit annoying.

So I decided to build a small application called Flu Reporter and share it with people who still want to use Google flu trend data. Read More
