Flu Reporter

During past few years especially in flu seasons, I used to use a Google web service to check the flu trend in Sydney area. After Google stopped this service on September 2015, I realized similar data still could be found on Google Trend. However, you have to set category, country, region, time … to get result. That’s a bit annoying.

So I decided to build a small application called Flu Reporter and share it with people who still want to use Google flu trend data. Read More
